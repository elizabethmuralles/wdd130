White Rapids Adventure

At White Rapids Adventure, we are passionate about providing thrilling and unforgettable rafting experiences. Our team of certified and experienced guides is dedicated to ensuring your safety and fun on every trip.

History

White Rapids Adventure began over two decades ago as a small family-operated rafting club. What started as weekend river trips quickly grew into one of the most recognized adventure companies in the region.

Today, we continue our mission of connecting people with nature through unforgettable outdoor experiences while ensuring safety, excellence, and sustainability in everything we do.
